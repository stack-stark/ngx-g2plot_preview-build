    • 介绍

    ngx-g2plot是一个基于@antv/g2plot封装的angular指令库。恰到好处的封装使得使用起来很方便,让开发者专注业务开发。

    Ngx-g2plot is an angular instruction library encapsulated based on @antv/g2plot. Proper encapsulation makes it easy to use and allows developers to focus on business development.
